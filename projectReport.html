Crime rate peaks during lunch hour and rush hours in San Francisco

Problem discription

The goal of this study is to visualize the data of crimes in San Francisco area, and show that the crime rate reaches peak during lunch hour and rush hoursin San Francisco. The pattern of the crime is relatively consistent across the summer months (June, July and Auguest) in 2014.

Supporting evidence from the data

The first supporting evidence is that, in general, the crime rates peaks around 12:00pm, which is the lunch hour; and around 17:00pm and 1 hour after that, which is the rush hour.

ggplot(sf_inc, aes(x=as.factor(hours))) + geom_bar()

More detailed data shows that the crime patterns during lunch hour and rush hour are relatively consistent, which theft the most common. In the plot below, peakHour12 stands for the crime pattern during its peak hour around 12:00pm (lunch hours), and peakhour1718 stands for the crime pattern during its peak hour around 17:00pm and 18:00pm (rush hours).

 ggplot(peakHour12, aes(x=peakHour12)) + geom_bar()+ coord_flip()

 ggplot(peakHour1718, aes(x=peakHour1718)) + geom_bar()+ coord_flip()

To show more details, although the occurrence of theft seems to have some moderate increase from June to July, it appears that the crime patterns also keep relatively consistent across different months in summer (June, July, Augest). In the plot below, the post-fixes after peakHour12_ and peakHour1718_ like June, July and Aug indicate the month.

 ggplot(peakHour12_June, aes(x=peakHour12_June)) + geom_bar()+ coord_flip()

 ggplot(peakHour12_July, aes(x=peakHour12_July)) + geom_bar()+ coord_flip()

 ggplot(peakHour12_Aug, aes(x=peakHour12_Aug)) + geom_bar()+ coord_flip()

 ggplot(peakHour1718_June, aes(x=peakHour1718_June)) + geom_bar()+ coord_flip()

 ggplot(peakHour1718_July, aes(x=peakHour1718_July)) + geom_bar()+ coord_flip()

 ggplot(peakHour1718_Aug, aes(x=peakHour1718_Aug)) + geom_bar()+ coord_flip()

Summary

The crime rate peaks during lunch hours and rush hours in San Francisco area, and the crime pattern is consistent in this two time slots across the three summer months.
